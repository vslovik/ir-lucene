Did You Know?

Trivia

Universal's director short list for the project consists of Frank Darabont, Gavin O'Connor, and Andrés Muschietti. Darabont was eventually signed for the directorial duties for the project, but dropped out of the film. Darabont stayed on as a co-writer, he was later replaced by Cedric Nicolas-Troyan who worked on Snow White and the Huntsman (2012) as visual effect supervisor and second unit director. See more »
